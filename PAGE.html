About Us

Welcome to HADIQATUS SIBYAN SCHOOL OF SCIENCE & QUR'AN, a leading educational institution committed to providing a nurturing and inclusive learning environment for students of all backgrounds.


Our Mission

Our mission is to nurture well-rounded individuals who excel academically, embody Islamic values, and contribute positively to society. We strive to create an environment where students can achieve their full potential while staying true to their faith.


Our Vision

We envision a future where our students are leaders and role models in their communities, equipped with knowledge, wisdom, and a strong moral compass. Our goal is to inspire a love for lifelong learning and a commitment to upholding Islamic principles.

We invite you to visit our school, meet our staff, and experience our unique educational environment.
